Frankenmuth Event Prep & Travel FAQ

Planning around dinners, festivals, and the drive to Caro

When should I book if I need event-ready nails or lashes and I'm coming from Frankenmuth?

For weddings, Oktoberfest, Christmas market weekends, and other high-demand dates, book 2-3 weeks ahead. For routine appointments, 1 week ahead is usually enough. Plan your drive to Caro (about 30 minutes from Frankenmuth) so you have extra time before your dinner, photos, or event check-in.

How do I get to Charming Beauty from Frankenmuth?

Take M-83 north through Birch Run to Caro - it's about 30 minutes. We're at 124 N. State St with free parking on the street and behind the building. Much easier to park than downtown Frankenmuth!

Can I book before my Zehnder's or Bavarian Inn dinner?

That's one of our most popular requests. Book your nail and lash appointment in the morning or early afternoon, then head to Frankenmuth for dinner looking your best. We recommend booking at least a week ahead for dinner date appointments.

Do you book appointments for Oktoberfest weekend?

Yes - Oktoberfest week is busy! Book at least 2-3 weeks ahead if you want nails and lashes for the festival. We can usually fit both services in the same appointment.

Is it better to come here or go to a Saginaw salon?

Many Frankenmuth residents choose us over Saginaw salons. The drive to Caro is similar (or shorter depending on traffic), parking is free and easy, and you get personalized one-on-one service instead of a busy chain salon.
