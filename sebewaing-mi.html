Sebewaing FAQs: Travel Time, Booking, and Best Service Combos

How do I get to Charming Beauty from Sebewaing?

Take M-25 south to Unionville, then M-81 west to Caro. It's about 35 minutes total. We're at 124 N. State St with free parking on the street and behind the building.

Do you book appointments before Sugar Festival weekend?

Yes! Sugar Festival week is one of our busiest times for Sebewaing clients. Book at least 2-3 weeks ahead for the best availability. We can usually fit in nails and lashes on the same day.

Will gel polish hold up at the lake?

Yes - that's why Sebewaing clients love gel manicures and pedicures. The polish won't chip from water, sand, or boat activities. Most clients get 2-3 weeks of wear through summer lake days.

Can I book appointments for USA school events?

Absolutely! We do prom, homecoming, and graduation prep for Unionville-Sebewaing Area students. For groups, book at least 2-3 weeks in advance to get everyone scheduled together.

Why do Sebewaing clients choose Caro instead of Bay City?

Most clients tell us they want one trusted specialist and predictable results, not a different experience each visit. In Caro, they can combine lashes, nails, and brows in one appointment, park easily, and follow a straightforward 35-minute route. The convenience plus consistency makes the drive worthwhile.

What should I book if I only come once every 3-4 weeks?

The most popular plan is a lash fill plus gel manicure, or gel pedicure plus brow shaping. These combinations keep your routine simple between visits and help you get the most value from each drive from Sebewaing.

How long should I plan for a Sebewaing appointment day?

Plan about 35 minutes each way for driving. Most single services take 45-60 minutes, two-service appointments usually take about 90 minutes, and a full lash set with nails can take up to 2.5 hours. We can help sequence your booking around work or event times.
