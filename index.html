Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our nail salon, lash extensions & beauty services in Caro, MI

Where is Charming Beauty located in Caro, MI?

We're at 124 N. State St in downtown Caro, MI 48723 — inside Just Teasin' Salon. Free street parking is available out front, and there's a dedicated lot behind the building. Open Mon–Fri 9am–6pm and Sat 9am–4pm.

Do you offer lash extensions in Caro?

Yes — lash extensions are one of our most-booked services. Kate Tu has 5+ years of lash extension experience and offers classic, volume, and hybrid sets, plus lash lifts. Most clients come back for fills every 2–3 weeks. Learn more about lash extensions →

What nail services do you offer in Caro?

We offer gel manicures, custom nail art, nail extensions, dip powder, and pedicures (spa and gel). All nail services use OPI and Valentino products for long-lasting, chip-free results. See our nail services →

Do you do brow lamination in Caro?

Yes! Brow lamination gives you fluffy, brushed-up brows that hold their shape for 6–8 weeks. We also offer brow shaping and tinting. Many clients add a brow service to their nail or lash appointment. View brow lamination →

How far is Charming Beauty from Vassar?

We're a 15-minute drive from Vassar via M-46 west into Caro. No highway driving — straight shot with free parking when you arrive. See the Vassar page →

How far is Charming Beauty from Millington?

About 20 minutes north on M-15 from Millington to our Caro salon. A quick, straightforward drive with no highway. See the Millington page →

How do I book an appointment?

Book online anytime using our booking widget below, or call us at (989) 673-4699. Saturday morning slots fill the fastest — book ahead to get your preferred time.
