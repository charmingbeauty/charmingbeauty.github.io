Questions from Millington Clients

How do I get to Charming Beauty from Millington?

Take M-15 north for about 20 minutes until you reach Caro. We're located at 124 N. State St with free parking on the street and in the lot behind the building. It's a straight shot with no highway driving.

Can you fit in appointments for Millington Cardinals events like prom?

Yes! We book up fast for prom and homecoming season. For the best availability, book your nail and lash appointments 2-3 weeks before the event. We can usually accommodate same-week requests for smaller services like brow shaping.

How often do Millington lash clients come in for fills?

Most of our Millington clients schedule lash fills every 2-3 weeks. We recommend booking your next fill before you leave so you get your preferred time slot. Fill appointments take 45-60 minutes.

What are the best appointment times for working Millington clients?

Saturday mornings (9am-12pm) are most popular with working clients. We're also open Monday-Friday 9am-6pm if you can swing a lunch break or after-work appointment. Book online anytime at your convenience.
